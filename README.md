# Lefa's Car Rental — Website

A modern, responsive, and fully functional website for **Lefa's Car Rental**, a family-owned business based in Gaborone and Palapye, Botswana. The site showcases their two core service offerings: reliable car rentals and party/event equipment hire.

---

## 🚗 About the Project

Lefa's Car Rental provides affordable, clean, and reliable vehicles for daily use, weekend trips, and business travel. In addition, they offer a wide range of fun equipment for events and parties, including quad bikes, trampolines, jumping castles, paintball, and more.

This website was built to give the business a professional online presence, making it easy for customers to find services, contact the team, and book rentals.

---

## 🎯 Features

- **Responsive Design** – Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Dual Service Display** – Clearly separates car rental and event/party equipment services.
- **Car Fleet Showcase** – Displays available vehicles with key features.
- **Party Equipment Gallery** – Highlights fun offerings like quad bikes, jumping castles, and paintball.
- **Interactive Contact Form** – Allows customers to send inquiries directly.
- **WhatsApp Integration** – One-click contact via WhatsApp for quick bookings.
- **Location-Specific Contact Info** – Separate contact details for Gaborone and Palapye.
- **Clean, Modern UI** – Built with a professional, trustworthy aesthetic.
- **Fast Loading** – Lightweight and optimised for performance.
